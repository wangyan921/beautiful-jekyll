Short Bio

I am now a Postdoc Researcher with Computer Cognition, Vision, and Learning Research (CCVL) Group, John Hopkins University, advised by Alan Yuille. Before joining CCVL group, I was a Research Scholar with the Computational Biology and Cognitive Science (CBCS) Laboratory, The Ohio State University, advised by Aleix Martinez. I received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2017, advised by Prof. Alex Kot.
